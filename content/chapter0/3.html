The different flavors of ETFs
●
Sector and Industry ETFs:
Provide exposure to
an industry, such as pharmaceuticals, high-tech, or
commodities.
●
Style ETFs:
Track an investment style or market
capitalization focus, such as large-cap value or small
–cap growth.
●
Market ETFs:
Designed to track a particular index
like the S&P 500 or NASDAQ.
●
Bond ETFs:
Provide exposure to nearly every
type of bond available including U.S. Treasury,
Corporate, Municipal, International, High Yield and
more.
●
Commodity ETFs:
Track the price of a commodity,
such as oil, gold or corn.
